Entry Sections

Each edition is subdivided into alphabetical sections of different lengths. We further subdivide them into groups of 225 pages that we work on. We refer to these as sections, and identify each with the entry letter followed by a 2-digit number for each group in consecutive order. Thus section s01 is the first 225 pages in the letter "S," section b02 begins with the 226th page of "B," and r03 covers 451-675 of the letter "R."

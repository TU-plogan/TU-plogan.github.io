General Tab

Settings for the General tab in the ABBYY FineReader Options window.

To access the Options windown, select Tools > Options....

  1. Open the General tab
  2. Select When OCR Editor starts > Open a new OCR project.

  3. Click OK when finished, to save all settings in the Options window.

Unnumbered Pages

Blank pages and pages with only illustrations require special treatment.

All editions have blank pages and full-page illustrations with captions. Do you include them in the output or not? It depends on whether or not the pages are numbered.
  • If the page is not numbered, check that the previous and following pages are numbered sequentially. If they are, then you should skip the pages completely.
  • If the page is numbered, then it has to be included in the output to keep the page numbering correct. Draw a small box in an empty area and let ABBYY FineReader recognize it.

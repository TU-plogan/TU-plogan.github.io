eb03 Leading Words

eb03 puts an extra word at the bottom of the page that should not be included in text boxes.

Printers in the eighteenth-century used to add the first word of the second page at the bottom of the first page, to insure that printed forms were bound in the right order. We do not want to include this word in our digital edition, because it would mean it appears twice. When drawing boxes, do not include these leading words.

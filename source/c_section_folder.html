Section Folders

The use and structure of section folders.

A section is a group of page images for up to 250 pages within one letter. We use sections to organize the OCR process into feasible sized OCR Projects. Each section is contained in a single folder. The naming convention for section folders is to begin with the entry letter, followed by a two-digit number referring to the first, second, third, and so on, sequence of 150-250 pages of the print text: a01, a02, a03, â¦ .

Note: Filenames with section folders do not refer to page numbers in the print volumes. Each section beging with the number 1, rather than the page number.

Section folders contain subfolders for the different types of production files we generate, preceded by a numeral that relects their position in the production sequence. They also contain the page-inventory file for the section.

Entry Sections

Each edition is subdivided into alphabetical sections of different lengths. We further subdivide them into a standard unit of 250 pages that we work on at a time. We refer to these subsections by using the entry letter followed by a 2-digit number for each 250-page slice. Thus s01 is the first 250-pages of the letter "S." b02 is pages 251-500 of "B," and r03 is pages 501-750 of the letter "R."

TEI

We use the scheme because of its widespread acceptance for archival storage of textual data. This is a complex scheme that encodes the structure of a document, rather than its display characteristics. It allows us to define what is a title, subtitle, and body text. It does not tell us which typeface or font size to use. Later, users can display those elements in the way that best suits their purposes.

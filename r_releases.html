    Machine-readable transcriptions of historical editions of the Encyclopedia Britannica

    We are releasing full-text versions in plain-text and TEI-XML formats of historical editions of the , suitable for text mining. Download links are posted on the individual edition pages as releases become available.

    The collection includes four editions, from 1797-1911.1 The print editions are:

    • 3rd ed. 18 vols. + 2 suppl. vols. Edinburgh: A. Bell and C. MacFarquhar, 1788-1797.
    • 7th ed. 21 vols. Edinburgh: Adam and Charles Black, 1830-1842.
    • 9th ed. 25 vols. NY: Charles Scribner's Sons, 1875-1889. (Authorized edition.)
    • 11th ed. 29 vols. NY: Cambridge University Press, 1910-1911.
